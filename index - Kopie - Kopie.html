About Me

Hi, I’m Nicolas – a data and digital strategy specialist with a background in economics, a passion for analytics, and a drive to make technology more human-centered and purpose-driven.

With over five years of experience across international organizations and complex digital ecosystems, I combine analytical precision with a deep understanding of user experience, communication, and cross-functional collaboration.

My academic foundation includes a Master of Science and Bachelor of Arts in Business and Economics from the University of Basel, with international studies in China and the UK. I focus on behavioral economics, health systems, and policy analysis—shaping how I approach data and decisions.

Core Competencies

  • Digital tracking systems (JavaScript, GTM)
  • Dashboards & BI (Power BI, Looker Studio)
  • Data engineering (Snowflake, SQL)
  • A/B testing & UX optimization
  • Agile project leadership (Jira, Confluence)

What I Bring

I bring a combination of technical skill and strategic insight to every project. I’m fluent in the technical “how” and the strategic “why” behind data, and I enjoy translating complex datasets into clear, actionable insights that support real business outcomes.
