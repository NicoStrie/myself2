About myself

I was born in Basel and raised in Ticino, which gave me the privilege of growing up between two cultures and languages from an early age. My curiosity for the world naturally led me to explore new perspectives, and eventually sparked a deep passion for learning Chinese.

This passion brought me first to a summer school at East China Normal University in Shanghai, where I immersed myself in intensive Chinese language and cultural lessons. Later, after completing my Master’s degree in Business and Economics at the University of Basel, I returned to Shanghai for a more advanced, intensive Chinese language program, paired with an internship experience in Guangzhou.

Today, while my professional journey revolves around analytics, data, and digital strategy, I continue to nurture my personal passions. I’m a proud new father, which means my free time is now both precious and rare...but when I can, I enjoy swimming and unwinding with a good movie.

My journey has been shaped by a love for learning, an openness to new experiences, and a commitment to personal and professional growth; values I carry with me in everything I do.
