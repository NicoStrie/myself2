About me

Hi, I’m Nicolas – a data and digital strategy specialist with a background in economics, a passion for analytics, and a drive to make technology more human-centered and purpose-driven.

With over five years of experience across international organizations and complex digital ecosystems, I combine analytical precision with a deep understanding of user experience, communication, and cross-functional collaboration.

My academic foundation includes a Master of Science and Bachelor of Arts in Business and Economics from the University of Basel, complemented by international study programs in China and the UK. During my studies, I developed a strong interest in behavioral economics, health systems, and policy analysis, which continues to shape the way I approach data and decision-making today.

Professionally, I’ve led and supported analytics and digital transformation projects in sectors such as automotive (Emil Frey), industrial manufacturing (Georg Fischer), luxury goods (Digital Luxury Group), and e-commerce. My work has included:

  • Implementing robust digital tracking systems and data layers (JavaScript, GTM)
  • Developing dashboards and performance reports (Power BI, Looker Studio, Snowflake, SQL)
  • Running A/B tests and UX optimizations
  • Advising internal and external stakeholders on campaign and product performance
  • Leading agile project teams using tools like Jira and Confluence

I bring a combination of technical skill and strategic insight to every project, with fluency in both the technical implementation and the "why" behind the numbers. I enjoy translating complex data into actionable insights that are easy to communicate and align with broader business goals.
